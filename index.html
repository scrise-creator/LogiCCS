About the Project

LogiCCS addresses one of the key challenges for large-scale carbon capture and storage deployment: developing efficient and sustainable maritime logistics infrastructure for CO₂ transport in the Nordic and Baltic region.


The project explores future transport chains, infrastructure needs, system integration, sustainability aspects and regional cooperation required to enable cost-efficient CCS deployment.
